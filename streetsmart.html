Street Smart

Unity | Simulation | Management | Puzzle | PC | WIP

Project Description

Street Smart is traffic management simulation puzzle serious game centered around active transportation city planning and pedestrian movement set in the Filipino context. This game's serious goal is to advocate for active transportation as a contemporary solution Philippine traffic through active infrastructure planning.

This game is an academic project current in the process of development for the courses in Capstone project series. I grouped up with 4 other students and we formed a group called Capstoners to develop this game. We are currently in the process of taking the final course in the Capstone Project series, as such the game is still work in progress.
