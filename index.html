I'm Okay - Global Game Jam 2023 Judges' Choice

I'm Okay is a 2D topdown horror maze game where a girl wakes up and realizes she's trapped in a dilapidated maze filled with obstacles and thorny vines. She must escape while being tormented by a horrifying creature and a malignant entity.

Street Smart

Street Smart is traffic management simulation puzzle serious game centered around active transportation city planning and pedestrian movement set in the Filipino context. This game's serious goal is to advocate for active transportation as a contemporary solution to Philippine traffic through active infrastructure planning.
