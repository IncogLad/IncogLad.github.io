Project Description

Renfield: Bring Your Own Blood is an action roguelike bullet hell game inspired by movie events in Renfield by Universal Pictures. This game offers simplicity in its gameplay and gets intense as you progress along the game with its unique upgrades, stages, bosses and weapons.

This game got released when I was worked on it as an Intern/Junior Engineer.

Roles & Contributions

My role in this project is a junior engineer. As a junior engineer, I was in charge of accomplishing assigned tasks from my senior engineers and producer.
