MagiHimeDive

Unity | Visual Novel | Puzzle | Match-3 | PC

Project Description

MagiHimeDive is a game that tells the life of a person with gacha addiction and possibly depression. To tell this story, the game is split into two halves: a visual novel portion that follows the main character going through daily life in the real world, and a puzzle game with scripted gacha elements that the main character plays in his downtime.

This game is an academic project developed for the courses in IEPROJ series. I grouped up with 6 other students and we formed a group called Otaconnect to develop this game.
